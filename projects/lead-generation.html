How It Works

1. Lead Collection

The system monitors multiple data sources:

  • Google Sheets (manual entry or CSV imports)
  • Web scraping from job boards, directories
  • API integrations (Apollo.io, LinkedIn Sales Navigator)
  • Webhook triggers from web forms

2. AI Qualification

Each lead is analyzed by Groq's Llama 8.1 model:

  • Company size and industry match
  • Pain point alignment with your solution
  • Decision-maker identification
  • Budget and timeline indicators

3. Scoring & Prioritization

Leads receive a composite score (0-100) based on:

  • AI Confidence: How well they match your ideal customer profile
  • Engagement Signals: Website visits, content downloads, social activity
  • Firmographic Data: Company size, revenue, growth indicators
  • Manual Overrides: Custom rules for specific industries or scenarios

4. Personalized Outreach

For qualified leads (score > 70):

  • AI generates custom email copy based on lead context
  • Subject lines optimized for open rates
  • Personalization using company info, recent news, pain points
  • Follow-up sequences with intelligent timing

5. Delivery & Tracking

Automated email sending via Gmail API with:

  • Rate limiting (avoid spam filters)
  • Open and click tracking
  • Reply detection and routing
  • Automatic follow-ups for non-responders
